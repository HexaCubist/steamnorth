Our Locomotives

Johnny

Johnny is a diesel driven locomotive built in 1961, and was brought to Whangarei in 1990.

The Price

The Price is a diesel driven locomotive built in 1961, and was donated to us in 2004.

Seymour

Seymour is our steam locomotive and was built in 1955, with an extensive overhaul and polish in 2007. It operates every third Sunday as well as on our other live days and special occasions.

Jiggers

Jiggers are used on some of the less-used tracks and can carry a party of 6 comfortably per cart.
